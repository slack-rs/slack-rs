fn handshake(self) -> Result<TlsStream<S>, HandshakeError<S>>

Restarts the handshake process.

If the handshake completes successfully then the negotiated stream is returned. If there is a problem, however, then an error is returned. Note that the error may not be fatal. For example if the underlying stream is an asynchronous one then HandshakeError::Interrupted may just mean to wait for more I/O to happen later.
